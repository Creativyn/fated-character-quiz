# Fated Character Quiz

## Official Soundtrack Design Bible

Version: 1.0

---

# Philosophy

The music of Fated should feel like it comes from a single world.

Each character has their own musical identity, but every piece should feel like another chapter of the same soundtrack.

The music should emphasize:

- warmth
- wonder
- whimsy
- mystery
- fantasy
- hope
- emotional sincerity

Avoid:

- EDM
- modern pop
- horror
- heavy action
- excessive percussion
- electronic dance sounds

The player should feel transported into another world.

# Audio Layers

Every personality consists of four independent layers.

Ambient

Background atmosphere during the cinematic.

Reveal

Played when the dominant personality appears.

Tick

Used while revealing percentage bars and secondary personalities.

Final

The fate confirmation sound.

---

# Mixing Philosophy

Ambient should never overpower dialogue.

Reveal should be noticeable without startling the player.

Tick sounds should remain subtle.

Final should provide emotional closure.

Silence is preferable to excessive sound.

---

## Silence

Silence is an instrument.

Do not fear moments where only the wind,
the water,
or a single sustained note is heard.

These moments allow the player to breathe,
to reflect,
and to connect emotionally with the world.

In Fated,
silence is not emptiness.

It is presence.

# Future Expansion

Additional personalities should follow this document.

Every new character should include:

- Theme title
- Emotional identity
- Key
- Tempo
- Instrument palette
- Ambient
- Reveal
- Tick
- Final

to preserve a unified musical identity throughout the Fated universe.
